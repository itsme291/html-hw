Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that souther hemisphere tends to be warmer than northern hemisphere this time of the year. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time the data was gathered.
